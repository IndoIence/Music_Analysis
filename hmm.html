About

Genius Annotation

“1949” is an unreleased “Lolita” inspired song by Lana Del Rey, in the Lizzy Grant era. It is speculated to have been recorded in 2008. The song leaked on July 1, 2012, and is partly a demo for “Every Man Gets His Wish”. A studio version was leaked on May 11, 2016. This version abruptly cuts off at the end of the track and while it may be an extended snippet, it is also possible that the recording was never finished.
